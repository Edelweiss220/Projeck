PRIMAL SURVIVAL : PLANET TRIFFORA

THE BALCK EATH CALL

Planet Triffora is a tense and exciting FPS/MMORPG game set in a sci-fi universe in the future. The player will have to join one of the three fractions - colonists, local natives or intelligent animals - to fight for survival in an aggressive environment.

Triffora World

The basic game mechanics of Primal Survival: Planet Tiffora

Pick one of the three fractions.
After landing on Triffora, Colonists from old Terra came across tribes of local natives accustomed to the difficulties of a never-ending struggle for survival against local fauna that was rapidly evolving and actively developing an intelligent mind
Choose a weapon to fit your needs..
Firearms, lasers, hybrids, electromagnetic weapons, and other weapons developed significantly as a result of the Great Global War, but the colonists were surprised by the unique materials they found on Triffora.
Develop and learn new skills.
One must constantly improve their skills and abilities, both physical and spiritual, in order to survive in the planet Triffora's environment.
Craft new items and equipment
On their mission to colonize the planet Triffora, the ship was packed with the best equipment, but it had its limits. Surviving on the planet would require local manufacturing.
Use it or let yourself be used.
Triffora is inhabited by animals, some of which are almost as intelligent as humans. It is the high rate of mutation that helps them compete on equal terms with both the Colonists and the local aborigines.
Build your own home.
You won't be able to survive without shelter. The colonists will have to learn to use local materials to build houses, and, who knows, even entire cities.
Science and research.
The ability to quickly learn from strangers makes the local aborigines extremely dangerous opponents, but the Colonists must also study the characteristics of the local flora and fauna and develop new technologies to ensure their survival
Higher powers
A complete surprise for the Colonists was the presence of some higher power on the planet, the location of which is very difficult to predict. Local aborigines, through special rituals, actively use them for their protection and often for attack.
